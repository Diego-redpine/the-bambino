Nestled on Midvale's storied Main Street, The Bambino is where authentic wood-fired craftsmanship meets the warmth of family tradition. Every pizza that emerges from our oven carries with it decades of passion—and a nod to the cinematic magic filmed just steps from our door.

The Story

Our gallery wall celebrates the legends of baseball and film
"Heroes get remembered, but legends never die."
— The Sandlot

The Bambino isn't just a name—it's a tribute. A tribute to Babe Ruth, to the beloved film that brought his legend to life for a new generation, and to the very neighborhood where those iconic scenes were captured.

Walk through our doors and you'll find more than exceptional pizza. You'll discover walls adorned with memories: signed photographs from The Sandlot cast, vintage baseball memorabilia, and cherished family portraits that tell the story of our community.

This is a place where strangers become friends over shared plates, where children learn about legends, and where every meal is a celebration of the things that truly matter.
